## Growth Philosophy

PT grows when **predictions become conversations**. Every correct prediction is a brag. Every resolved market is a story. Every streak is a challenge. The platform's growth engine is built on social behavior — not paid acquisition.

**Primary growth mechanic:** Social proof + shareable moments → organic acquisition

---

## The 5 Core Growth Loops

### Loop 1 — The Prediction Share Loop
```
User makes a correct prediction
    → Platform generates shareable "Called It" card
        → User posts to X, Instagram, TikTok
            → Friend sees the card, gets curious
                → Friend joins PT
                    → Friend makes a prediction
                        → Loop restarts
```
**Key mechanic:** Shareable prediction cards (generated automatically on correct outcome)
**Viral coefficient goal:** Each user brings 0.3–0.5 new users via shares
**KPI:** Share rate on outcome reveals

---

### Loop 2 — The Streak Challenge Loop
```
User builds a 7-day streak
    → Streak visible on public profile
        → User mentions streak on social ("Day 7 on PT 🔥")
            → Friends challenge them or join to compete
                → Competition increases daily return rate
                    → Streaks attract more users
```
**Key mechanic:** Streaks + public profiles
**Retention impact:** Users with 7+ day streaks have 3–5x higher retention
**KPI:** Streak length distribution, day-7 retention rate

---

### Loop 3 — The Leaderboard Competition Loop
```
User sees they're ranked #52
    → Wants to reach top 10
        → Makes more predictions with higher conviction
            → Rank improves
                → User shares "I'm #8 globally in Crypto" moment
                    → New users join to challenge
```
**Key mechanic:** Category leaderboards + shareable rank cards
**Community effect:** Competition communities form around categories
**KPI:** Weekly active predictors, rank movement activity

---

### Loop 4 — The Content Engine Loop
```
PT posts viral market content (TikTok/X)
    → Viewers discover PT via social media
        → Convert to sign-ups
            → New users make predictions
                → Outcomes resolve → PT posts outcome content
                    → Previous viewers re-engage ("did the community get it right?")
                        → More sign-ups, more predictions
```
**Key mechanic:** Content engine (see CONTENT-ENGINE.md)
**Content → Conversion:** Goal 1-3% from content view to sign-up
**KPI:** Traffic from social content, content-attributed sign-ups

---

### Loop 5 — The Creator Economy Loop
```
A creator (X, YouTube, TikTok) builds a prediction record on PT
    → Shares their record with their audience
        → Audience follows them on PT
            → Creator becomes a "Featured Predictor"
                → Creator creates content about PT + their predictions
                    → PT gains new users through creator's audience
                        → Creator gains platform credibility
```
**Key mechanic:** Public profiles + creator verification + featured predictors
**Long-term:** Creator-audience relationships drive sustained inflow
**KPI:** Creator-attributed sign-ups, creator content volume

## Creator Economy Design

### Who Is a PT Creator?
Anyone who:
- Has a public PT profile
- Makes predictions publicly
- Has an audience anywhere (X, TikTok, YouTube, Twitch)
- Uses PT as part of their content strategy

PT does not require formal influencer programs at first — it's open by default.

---

### Creator Features

#### 1. Public Prediction Record
- All predictions public by default (toggle for private)
- Accuracy stats visible: overall, per category, per timeframe
- Prediction history with outcomes marked ✅ / ❌ / ⏳

#### 2. Creator Profile Card (shareable)
```
┌──────────────────────────────────────┐
│  🎯 @username on Prediction Trade    │
│  ─────────────────────────────────   │
│  Predictions: 124 | Accuracy: 68%    │
│  Best category: AI & Tech (74%)      │
│  Current streak: 🔥 21 days          │
│  Rank: #12 globally                  │
│  ─────────────────────────────────   │
│  predictiontrade.online/@username    │
└──────────────────────────────────────┘
```

#### 3. Featured Predictors Section
- `/leaderboard` features top predictors by category
- "Expert Voices" section on market detail pages
- "What are top predictors saying?" widget

#### 4. Creator Content Templates
PT provides ready-made templates for creators:
- "My prediction this week" card
- "My track record" card
- "I called this one" outcome card
- "Challenge me" card (invites followers to predict against them)

### Creator Economy Roadmap

**Phase 1 (now):** Public profiles + shareable cards
**Phase 2:** Creator verification + "Top Predictor" badge
**Phase 3:** Featured creator program (invite top predictors to be highlighted)
**Phase 4:** Creator tips / tipping (virtual points, not real money)

## Viral Mechanics

### Mechanics by Trigger

| Trigger | Mechanic | Social action |
|---|---|---|
| Correct prediction | "Called It" card | User shares to social |
| 7-day streak | Streak milestone badge | User brags on X/TikTok |
| Top 10 leaderboard | Rank achievement card | User posts rank |
| Market outcome | Outcome reveal | PT posts, users reshare |
| New badge earned | Badge notification | User shares badge |
| Category expert status | Expert badge | User adds to bio |
| First prediction | "First Blood" moment | Subtle, encouraging |

### Virality Amplifiers

1. **Time sensitivity:** "Market closes in 2 hours" → urgency → more shares
2. **Controversy:** 50/50 markets generate more debate → more shares
3. **Big outcome moments:** Major events (elections, championships) → mass sharing
4. **Underdog outcomes:** When 20% was correct → "I was in the 20%!" shares
5. **Streak endangerment:** "Lose your streak if you don't predict today" → notifications

## Retention System

### Daily Return Drivers

| Mechanic | Frequency | Why it works |
|---|---|---|
| Streak (don't break it) | Daily | Loss aversion |
| Trending markets change | Hourly | FOMO |
| Market outcome notifications | On resolution | Curiosity/excitement |
| Weekly leaderboard update | Weekly | Competition |
| "New market in your category" | On creation | Personalization |

### Notification Strategy (opt-in only)

| Notification | Trigger | Tone |
|---|---|---|
| Streak at risk | 20h without prediction | "Your streak is on the line." |
| Market resolved | Your market closes | "The result is in." |
| You were right | Correct outcome | "You called it. 🎯" |
| New trending market | High-volume new market | "This is moving fast." |
| Leaderboard change | You moved up | "You're now #8." |

## North Star Metric

**Weekly Active Predictors** — the number of unique users who made at least 1 prediction in the past 7 days.

This metric captures: engagement, retention, and product-market fit simultaneously.

Secondary metric: **Share Rate** — % of correct outcomes that result in a social share.
